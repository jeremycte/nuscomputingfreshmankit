2. What's BT1101, CS1010S, CS1010, MA1521 or IS1103?

    • NUS Mods is the best place to learn and understand what each module encompasses and covers while also understanding what each module code stands for! NUS Mods will be quite essential for planning your modules as there's a built-in timetable planner for you to plan out the semester 1 & 2.
    • Explore the modules based on your major (Your Degree e.g. Computer Science or Information Systems)
      • https://nusmods.com

In the coming weeks, you'll receive more information via your email (the email you registered when applying for NUS), so do keep a lookout for more information on module enrollment, events, talks, and the SoC Academic Open Day 😃
